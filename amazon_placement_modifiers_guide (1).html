7 Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Mistake #1: Using Default 0% Modifiers

Why it's bad: You're letting Amazon randomly distribute budget across placements with wildly different performance.

Fix: Calculate data-driven modifiers based on RPC.

‚ùå Mistake #2: One Campaign for All Placements

Why it's bad: Mixed performance data makes optimization impossible.

Fix: Separate campaigns by placement focus with aggressive modifiers.

‚ùå Mistake #3: Ignoring Product Pages

Why it's bad: PP often has the best ROAS due to low CPC, even with lower CVR.

Fix: Run dedicated PP campaigns for competitor targeting.

‚ùå Mistake #4: Focusing Only on ACOS

Why it's bad: A placement might have higher ACOS but better total ROAS due to volume.

Fix: Track Total Sales and ROAS alongside ACOS.

‚ùå Mistake #5: Not Testing Incrementally

Why it's bad: Massive modifier changes create unpredictable results.

Fix: Adjust modifiers in 50-100% increments, test for 1-2 weeks.

‚ùå Mistake #6: Changing Modifiers Without Data

Why it's bad: You're optimizing blind.

Fix: Run campaigns for at least 2 weeks, gather 50-100 clicks per placement first.

‚ùå Mistake #7: Setting Modifiers Above 900%

Why it's bad: Amazon caps modifiers at 900%. Going higher does nothing.

Fix: If you need effective bid above 900%, raise your base bid instead.
